NPS Callbacks & Medallia Integration

Customer Timeline now makes it easier than ever to 1) find customers requesting a NPS callback via Medallia survey, 2) initiate a call and 3) submit feedback directly into Medallia without ever leaving Timeline! Account Search by Email

To provide more flexibility in account-search, Customer Timeline now supports for search by account email address (3rd party preferred email, comcast.net email and/or userid)

 

New Watchlist UX

Timeline Watchlist feature received a major facelift. Now you can maintain your Watchlists with a better user experience. Watchlist now offers the ability to configure “auto-remove” rules to age customers off your lists and enhanced user management and list sharing features.  Lastly, Watchlist is fully API-enabled to support application integration and dynamic list management via real-time services.

SmartConnect: Consent Program

SmartConnect is playing a critical technology role in gaining customer approval and keeping them informed on any changes to their package.  SmartConnect orchestrates any “consent fallout situations” where a customer’s bill may change and our omni-channel notification strategy is executed through X1 Notification Center, MyAccount App, SMS, and Email. SmartConnect also provides business continuity coverage in the event ConsentDB is down, triggering consent notifications on any new orders detected through BEP.

Product Security for Watchlist

Timeline Watchlist notifications have been enhanced to improve Product Security in multiple communication channels. Customer account numbers have been removed and only customer names will post to current (Email, Slack) and future messaging channels with deeplinks back to Customer Timeline details.

 

 

Timeline API Upgrade

The Timeline team paid down some serious technical debt by eliminating redundant application programming interfaces (APIs) that serve and support the platform.  Applications such as Timeline UI, Einstein360, xfinity.com and MyAccount Mobile App leverage Timeline Platform APIs millions of times per day to serve our customers and workforce.  As a result of this initiative the platform is more reliable, scalable, and performant to meet the current and future needs of the business.

Expiring Creditcard, First Bill Video

Two new SmartConnect use cases were deployed to production to help improve customer awareness and decrease customer call-in-rate (CIR).  Customer Credit Card Expiring service alert proactively notifies customers of a pending CC expiration and guides them to update auto-pay settings on MyAccount.  First Bill Video service alert presents on the my.xfinity.com Blue Bar and takes the customer to a video tutorial explaining their First Bill.  We expect approximately 3M customer alerts annually for these two use cases.

CX Virtual Assistant

Now customers can get answers, support and even recommendations from their own virtual customer experience assistant, 24/7/365 on the My Account app and later this year in other digital channels like xfinity.com and through our X1 Voice Remote.  Customer assistant inquiries and search activity are now ingested into Timeline, helping us understand customer digital behaviors and personalize the customer journey with every interaction.  Learn more here.

Timeline Wholesale

Timeline continues to drive benefits internally and is gaining interest externally.  We are in discussions with Comcast Ventures, NBCU, other MSOs to explore licensing and syndication opportunities.  As part of Lab Week we demoed a replication of Customer Timeline platform in a separate, multi-tenant cloud environment for a fictitious customer “CarCo”. The Timeline platform has proven scale, been extended to AWS Cloud and will able to support new tenants by May 2016.

Elements 2.0 Automated Ingest

Data engineering ingestion processes have been automated in Timeline Elements 2.0 which is on our new AWS cloud platform.  This technical innovation creates the ability to ingest new data sources into Timeline in MINUTES (formerly weeks or months) and transforms previous manual processing efforts from highly skilled data engineers into a streamlined, self-service web experience.

NPS Smart Callbacks

We want to make the NPS callback process more simple and effective, so we are building machine learning and real-time integration between Medallia and Timeline Talk to reduce employee effort and improve payback on our efforts. During Lab Week we released a proof-of-concept to show how employees could easily report on high-value callbacks, initiate the callback and complete notes without ever logging into Medallia.  Click here to see it. We expect a production release of the solution by May 2016.

Truck Roll Journey Visualization

Customers are in a Truck Roll Journey when they either have a technician appointment pending or had one within the last 30-days.  TR Journey Badge is now expanded to specific journey milestones and metrics giving users a simple visual to understand journey duration, rework, and customer effort.

Truck Roll Journey Outliers

The Truck Roll Journey Outlier Report highlights the 2,000 customers currently experiencing the longest, active truck roll journeys. This feature is in beta release as data quality across source systems will sometimes surface duration inaccuracies.  In partnership with EBI, we are exposing journey intelligence reporting and workforce performance reporting as additional tools to help leadership (HQ, Division, Region, Frontline) improve operations and the customer experience.

AppDynamics and Anodot

Timeline integrated two important technologies for big data platform operations, monitoring and data quality.  AppDynamics monitors platform components across the broad ecosystem of Timeline platform services and infrastructure.  Anodot is a real-time anomaly detection service that plugs into Timeline data streams and identifies patterns and statistical control thresholds to surface operational and data quality issues.

Customer Request (CR) Tickets

Timeline now offers Customer Request Tickets. CR tickets are logged in the Trouble Ticket System (TTS) when a customer has an issue such as a technical or billing problem, along with resolution steps.

SmartConnect: Billing Experience Credit

Comcast had a hiccup with incorrect billing information placed on over 1M customer accounts in late January.  Smart Connect was leveraged to provide call context for this error, alerting Agents about the problem for over 50K customers that saw incorrect balance information. CX and Finance teams drove a response to apologize to customers that paid an incorrect amount and offer them a credit on their next bill for the inconvenience.  Learn more on ComcastNow.
